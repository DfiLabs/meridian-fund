01. Overview

Our multi‑strategy program allocates capital across a small number of books and rebalances as conditions change.

Objective. Deliver uncorrelated crypto alpha via a stable return profile, clean execution and institutional governance.

We operate from a risk‑controlled foundation, but leave room for tactical directional exposure when our signals justify it.

Counterparty risk reduced. We use a ring‑fenced vehicle (Segregated Portfolio within a Segregated Portfolio Company), diversify venues and counterparties, cap exposures, keep idle balances minimal on exchanges, maintain optional third‑party custody for reserves and design flexible setups so we are never tied to one provider.

02. Strategy Books

ML Long/Short

Machine‑learning driven long/short in liquid markets (perpetual futures and/or spot‑perp). Models are refreshed daily to capture cross‑sectional momentum. Directional exposure can be taken when model confidence is high.

Basis & Opportunistic

Cash‑and‑carry or funding capture only when yields are attractive — often a few bursts in a year. When carry is thin, we stand down.

Gamma Harvesting

Short‑dated, hedged premium capture on top venues. Size is kept small with strict limits and fast adjustments.

Yield

Conservative yield on idle USD or stablecoins via tokenised T‑bills and custodial programs. Higher‑risk yield such as structured funding capture or secured lending is only employed when carry clearly exceeds costs and always within hard limits.

04. Allocation & Risk

  • How we decide: Daily oversight by portfolio managers and a weekly investment committee to size, rotate or pause books.
  • When we deploy capital: Books are only activated when the expected profit after costs (fees, funding or borrow, execution slippage) is clearly positive. If the edge fades, the book is closed and capital is moved elsewhere.
  • How we cut losses: Each book has preset loss checkpoints. When hit, we reduce or close that book promptly. Portfolio‑level limits sit on top.

05. Structure & Governance

  • Vehicle: Meridian Fund SP, a segregated portfolio within Atitlan SPC (Cayman). Statutory ring‑fencing of assets and liabilities, independent directors, AML officers and board‑approved policies (valuation, best execution, conflicts, cyber).
  • Service provider experience: We have already set up similar structures and are familiar with administrators, auditors, legal counsel, banks, custody and exchange onboarding workflows.
  • Manager in France: DFi Labs SAS, PSAN registration with the AMF (E2022‑032).
  • Operating setup: We can run prime‑brokered direct market access or a direct multi‑exchange setup with optional custody. We choose what maximises net returns while keeping counterparty exposure controlled.
